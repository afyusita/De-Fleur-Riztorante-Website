De Fleur Ristorante

De Fleur Ristorante is an Italian restaurant in Lenmarc Mall, West Surabaya

Established in December 2023, we have been serving authentic Italian menus; Pizza, Pasta, and the famous Bistecca. With the strong figure of Chef John Doe, an Italian-born Chef from Puglia region, Italy. We have become a place for family, friends and companies to enjoy their meals ranging from casual eats to special events, such as birthday celebrations and company gatherings.

    
Chef John Doe
John Doe, an Italian-born Chef from the Puglia region of Italy, has been leading our kitchen since December 2023. With a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, Chef John has brought his expertise and creativity to our restaurant, making it a beloved spot for family gatherings, friends' meetups, and special events. His signature dishes include our famous Pizza, Pasta, and Bistecca.
Chef Jane Smith
Jane Smith, a renowned chef from the Tuscany region of Italy, has been a culinary artist since January 2022. Her dedication to creating exquisite Italian dishes has made her a celebrated figure in our restaurant. Jane's specialties include handcrafted pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and her signature tiramisu.
