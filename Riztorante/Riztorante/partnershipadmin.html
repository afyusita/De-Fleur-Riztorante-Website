Our Service

We emphasize on our food & service quality.

CULINARY PARTNERSHIPS

A culinary partnership is a form of collaboration in which De Fleur Ristorante, a restaurant known for serving authentic Italian cuisine, collaborates with a famous chef or winner of a cooking competition to create a special menu that will be offered to customers during a predetermined partnership period. Through this partnership, De Fleur Ristorante will benefit from the chef's reputation, while the chef will get a platform to promote their culinary creativity to a wider audience. The terms and conditions of the partnership will include the duration of the agreement, special menu details, joint promotional strategies, as well as revenue sharing from sales of the special menu.

EVENT PARTNERSHIPS

Event partnerships refer to collaborations between De Fleur Ristorante and other local businesses or organizations to host special events that are of interest to customers. For example, a wine pairing event with a local wine shop or an art evening with a local gallery. In this partnership, the responsibilities and roles of each party will be clearly defined, including providing venues, food, event promotion, as well as sharing income from the event.

RESTAURANT COLLABS

Partnerships with other restaurants are a form of collaboration where De Fleur Ristorante collaborates with other restaurants that have a similar theme or culinary style to hold joint dining events or present unique crossover menus. Through this partnership, both restaurants will mutually benefit by expanding each other's customer base and increasing each other's brand exposure. The terms and conditions of the partnership will include the duration of the partnership, crossover menu details, joint promotional strategies, as well as revenue sharing from crossover menu sales.
